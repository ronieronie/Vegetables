Welcome to "Fruits and Vegetables Price Forecasting"

Our Vegetable and Fruits Price Forecasting System offers reliable price predictions for vegetables and fruits in Metro Manila and nearby provinces, based on data from the World Food Programme. By analyzing market trends, it helps farmers, traders, and consumers anticipate price changes, enabling smarter decisions for harvesting, selling, and budgeting.

PRICE PREDICTIONS

Our advanced forecasting system analyzes historical price data, seasonal patterns, weather conditions, and market trends to predict vegetable and fruit prices with average accuracy using Linear Regression. The system provides short-term and medium-term forecasts.


REGIONAL COVERAGE

Specialized focus on Metro Manila and surrounding provinces including Bulacan, Rizal, Laguna, and Cavite. Our system understands the unique market dynamics and supply chain patterns of the National Capital Region and its agricultural suppliers.


DATA-DRIVEN INSIGHTS

Utilizing machine learning algorithms and statistical models, our system processes vast amounts of market data, weather information, and economic indicators to generate reliable price forecasts for over 20 common vegetables and fruits.

COVERAGE AREAS
Our Vegetable and Fruits Price Forecasting System covers the major markets and trading centers across Metro Manila and strategically important nearby provinces.

METRO MANILA Primary coverage including major markets like Divisoria, Balintawak, and Marikina
BULACAN Agricultural supplier region with major vegetable production areas and significant crop yield potential.
RIZAL Key supply route province with important trading centers facilitating commerce.
LAGUNA Major fruit and vegetable growing region south of Metro Manila with abundant farms.
CAVITE Significant province playing a vital role in contributing to the NCR's food supply.
PAMPANGA Important agricultural region known for rice and vegetable production
